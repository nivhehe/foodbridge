How It Works

A clearer flow from surplus food to pickup

FoodBridge is designed to reduce confusion at every step, from posting available food to helping NGOs understand where, when, and how to collect it.

1

Restaurants post surplus

Share the food type, quantity, expiry window, and pickup location so opportunities are visible immediately.

2

NGOs review live listings

Browse active posts, understand the pickup details, and focus on the opportunities that fit current needs.

3

Communities benefit faster

Good food is redirected quickly, reducing waste while helping more people through a better coordination loop.

About FoodBridge

FoodBridge connects food businesses with community organizations in a way that feels more organized, more transparent, and easier to trust. The goal is simple: make food redistribution easier to act on.

Mission-led Reduce food waste while supporting local food access with a better digital workflow.
Practical Focus on the details that make pickup possible: quantity, expiry timing, and location context.
Action-oriented Guide users quickly from interest to posting, browsing, and follow-through.
